OUR RESTAURANT

At Zayka we follow strict standards on service, quality and presentation. We use only the finest ingredients in all of our dishes to ensure that every meal we serve is delicious and nutritious.

Zayka is also a picture perfect location for a romantic outing. The restaurant arranges a beautiful candle light dinner , which can be customized on the basis of choices and preferences. Also, if you happen to be at the restaurant on Sundays, which is highly recommended, do try the lavish Sunday Brunch at Zayka.

We encourage you to visit our restaurant and experience our cuisine. We look forward to your visit.
